## About MyParcel

Our mission is to deliver a world-class shipment platform to our customers. We believe technology can streamline the shipping process for e-commerce businesses.

## Tech Stack

MyParcel uses modern technologies:

- **PHP** and **Laravel**
- **Amazon Web Services**
- **TypeScript** and **JavaScript**
- **Vue.js**

Our team works with Agile methodologies, Infrastructure-as-Code with AWS CDK, and Trunk Based Development with comprehensive test coverage.

## Team

Our organization is built around specialized teams:

- **IT Support** — Internal systems management and support
- **Customer & New Product** — Customer-facing features and new product development
- **External Integrations** — Integrations with external platforms and partners
- **Shipment & Billing** — Core shipping and billing logic
- **Order & Fulfilment** — Order management and fulfilment processes
- **Cloud Engineering** — Infrastructure, CI/CD and platform stability
